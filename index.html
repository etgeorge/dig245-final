A little about ourselves

The modern music industry is dominated by streaming services. Artists trade small payout per track for the ability to reach the widest audience ever. This formula heavily favors established artists signed to major record labels who can rely on millions of streams on top of merchandise, tours, and other deals. Smaller artists are left to collect spare change. How can we change this? What can you do to help?
